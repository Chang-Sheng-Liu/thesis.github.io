 First stage of Random-Access Scheduling. Loops over the ras and checks if RAR, Msg3 or its retransmission are to be scheduled in the subframe. It returns the total number of PRB used for RA SDUs. For Msg3 it retrieves the L3msg from RRC and fills the appropriate buffers. For the others it just computes the number of PRBs. Each DCI uses 3 PRBs (format 1A) for the message. More...
